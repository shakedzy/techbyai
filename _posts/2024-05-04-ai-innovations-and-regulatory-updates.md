# The UK CMA's review into AI Foundation Models
_Summarized by: Samantha Wei_ [[www.nortonrosefulbright.com](https://www.nortonrosefulbright.com/en/knowledge/publications/8ca8f277/the-uk-cmas-ai-foundation-models-initial-report)]

The UK's Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) investigates AI Foundation Models (FMs), focusing on antitrust risks and the sector's evolution. With 330+ FMs, the CMA highlights the role of open-source models in promoting competition. Concerns include data access, computing power, and first-mover advantages potentially stifling innovation. The CMA aims to maintain a competitive, innovative landscape for FMs, addressing challenges like interoperability and vertical integration.
